How to Play

It's a game of Guess Who for flags!

Guess the FLAGLE in 6 tries.

A guess is when you click on a color button or a flag.

Press the color buttons to filter whether or not the FLAGLE contains the colors.

Example:
  • The FLAGLE does NOT contain orange
  • The FLAGLE contains green
You win by:
  • Clicking on the correct flag OR
  • Only the FLAGLE remains unfiltered
